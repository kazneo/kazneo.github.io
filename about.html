What truly drives me is working at the intersection of technology and strategy. I thrive in environments where I can analyze complex systems, identify growth opportunities, and bridge the gap between technical teams and business stakeholders.

For me, technology isn't just about code; it's a powerful tool for enabling people and transforming processes. I'm endlessly curious about how emerging trends in AI, quantum computing, and infrastructure can reshape industries and solve problems we haven't even defined yet.
